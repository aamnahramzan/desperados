Development Update

by Faheem Masjeed in March 28, 2021

Our last released build for the game was the MVP product that we released last year to showcase the basic gameplay and to get feedback from you, our community. In the months since then we have worked extremely hard to polish and develop the game further in all aspects. The experience now is significantly improved and we can’t wait for you to get your hands on it soon. In this blogpost we wanted to highlight some of those changes and differences in the game you can expect to see.

The first noticeable difference is the updated menus and in-game graphics. From new assets and backgrounds to improved menus, a lot of the visual identity of the game has been refined and updated. There are new textures for asteroids, background changes, adding of swappable smoke trails from the ship, and more.

Also many small changes and updates have been made to the balance and the feel of the game. From the shooting to the movement, a lot has been improved. Small changes like a new aiming reticule, a flip manoeuvre, and more responsive movement have made the gameplay feel more refined and smoother. In addition to this we have added a new feature that will allow you to locate an opponent near you, so that you can always find someone to engage with. This feature has increased the pace of the game and will cause players to rethink their approach to combat encounters.

Finally, the shop has now been implemented fully and you can buy upgrades after reaching a certain level. You will then be able to change the colour of your ship’s smoke trail to various different colours, and be able to do the same for the colour of the ship’s bullets. This will increase customisability and personalisation once you reach higher levels, rewarding those players who level up. Thank you for reading and we hope to provide you with further updates soon.
