Potential secuirity issues?

by Mohammed Zaakir Khan in Dec 10th, 2020

One main cybersecurity issue within games is data theft. The accounts are what is valuable to hackers, online games have a huge amount of data on the users, and that information is all personal and confidential information. They can do things like tracking, get access to monthly subscriptions and so much more.

Another thing would be phishing, its not only fraudulent emails, but it also goes even beyond that. They even would send links on in game chats, make fake accounts, posing as friends you name it. If a hacker were successful in phishing, they could even steal a whole gamers STEAM account!

"
If you put a key under the mat for the cops, a burglar can find it, too. Criminals are using every technology tool at their disposal to hack into people’s accounts. If they know there’s a key hidden somewhere, they won’t stop until they find it.
Tim Cook

How to stay protected?

Firstly, use password protection and using strong passwords will make it harder for hackers to get though. You should aim to change your passwords every 2 - 4 weeks which will make it even harder for hackers to get in.

Get malware protection. Find a good anti-virus you can install, finding one will allow protection from malware and anti-viruses, so your gaming can be kept safe and protection from any random attacks which is crucial.

Multi-factor authentication: games should have 2 factor authentications. If they give you the option, ALWAYS USE IT. This gives an extra layer of security to your games accounts etc. this will keep it a lot more safe and not vulnerable from hackers.

4. Be able to offer and accept constructive criticism. Not overtly negative comments, but feedback that enriches the process and helps you improve what you’ve done in a targeted manner.

Phishing awareness: phishing is serious, never click a link without knowing what it is or where it is going to go, those links can be malicious and cause real damage. One thing to remember, NEVER OPEN LINKS that is one of the number 1 rules to follow.
