Our Mission

Survival Space is an exciting multiplayer experience that lets you verse multiple opponents, all in real time. With controls easy to understand, it allows you to jump right in, so you will not miss any of the action. We give you control of a personalised warship that you can adapt and upgrade as the battle rages giving you a unique experience every time you play. Compete over several unique maps and game modes that change the way you play forcing you to adapt to a changing environment.

This game is perfect for children between the ages of 10 – 15-year olds as they can pick up the game within minutes and start planning tactics to progress to the next level. It helps build communication and teamwork skills as it is a team based multiplayer game so keeping your team member safe is vital.

Join your friends on a exciting, new space themed adventure.
