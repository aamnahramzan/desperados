Grahpics

by Jamshed Ali in February 10, 2021

In regards to the graphics of the game, we really wanted something that would stand out, something that would be quite memorable for our target audience. We choose darker themes for the background because we believe it accurately reflects what our game was about and with the dark sky and medium to lose lighting I believe we have achieved that.

With the ship designs, we wanted something that was not only visually appealing, but quite authentic and resembles something from a science fiction movie that is why as a team we chose a 3D game instead because it is more visually appealing than 2D and the bullets we also wanted to give a real feel to. Something that we paid particular attention to was explosions. the explosions were a particular tough feature to put in and make visual appealing. They were a feature we put in to add a "wow" factor.
